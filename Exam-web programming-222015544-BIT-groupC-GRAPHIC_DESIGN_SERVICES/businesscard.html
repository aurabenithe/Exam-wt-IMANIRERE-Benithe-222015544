Business Card Design Q&A

What is custom business card design and what will I get?

Forget boring business card templates! Each business card created by designers on DesignCrowd is tailored and unique for your business. Designers work with your logo and other design assets to provide business card design ideas that you will love.

Once your project is complete, you'll receive all the files you need to print your business cards. This includes print ready vector files and a web preview file, like a JPG on PNG. Of course, you also get full legal copyright of the design.

Does my business need a custom business card?

Of course! In the age of the smartphone, little cardboard rectangles with your name on might seem like an outdated mode of communication. But there's a good reason personalized business cards are still around: they work.

Anyone who has been to a networking event knows how important a great business card design can be to stand out from the crowd. It is an extension of your brand and is crucial in promoting your message long after you've left a meeting.

How do I get a business card design from DesignCrowd?

Simply launch a project on DesignCrowd, choose your budget and tell us what you'd like to see on your business card by completing your creative brief.

Work with DesignCrowd's world wide community of expert designers to find inspirational ideas for a business card that will deliver a personal touch.

What do I need to do to get an amazing business card for my business?

Write a clear and detailed business card design brief, provide any required assets (such as a logo, company colors etc) and set your budget. During the creative journey provide constructive, direct feedback to designers to ensure you get the perfect business card design for you.

The more details you provide in your business card design brief and the more feedback you provide, the better the results!
